# Monogatari 🌸

### Every learner has a story. Start yours.

Monogatari is an AI-powered Japanese learning platform designed to transform language learning into a personal journey.

Instead of simply memorizing vocabulary and grammar rules, Monogatari helps learners build their own Japanese story through interactive lessons, AI-assisted practice, and personalized progress tracking.

From learning your first Hiragana character to having your first Japanese conversation, every step becomes part of your journey.

---

# ✨ Features

## 🔐 Personalized Learning Experience

Create your own learning profile and track your progress as you advance through your Japanese journey.

Features include:

* Secure user authentication
* Personalized dashboards
* Learning progress tracking
* Saved learning history

---

# 🌸 Kana Journey

Begin your Japanese adventure with the foundation of the language.

* Interactive Hiragana and Katakana charts
* Character learning support
* Beginner-friendly introduction to Japanese writing systems

---

# 📚 Vocabulary Growth

Expand your Japanese vocabulary through an interactive learning system.

* Vocabulary cards
* Word review practice
* Progress tracking
* Organized learning materials

---

# 🤖 AI Japanese Sentence Analyzer

Understand Japanese beyond translations.

The AI assistant helps learners:

* Break down Japanese sentences
* Explain grammar structures
* Identify vocabulary
* Understand sentence context and meaning

# 💬 AI Conversation Partner

Practice Japanese naturally through AI-powered conversations.

Features:

# 📊 Learning Dashboard

Your personal space to follow your progress.

Track:

* Completed lessons

# 🎨 Design Philosophy

Monogatari follows a **Modern Japanese-inspired aesthetic** focused on creating a calm, immersive, and enjoyable learning experience.

The design combines:

* Minimal modern interfaces
* Japanese-inspired visual elements
* Soft and welcoming aesthetics
* Smooth animations and interactions

The goal is to make Japanese learning feel less like studying and more like exploring a new world.
